We started Lumé with one belief: makeup and skincare should celebrate the skin you're in — never hide it.

Founded in California in 2021, Lumé Beauty is an independent brand built around clean, high-performance formulas and shades designed for real skin in real light. Every product is vegan, cruelty-free, and made without the long list of ingredients you'd rather avoid.

From a single matte lipstick to a full curated look, we obsess over the details — the wear, the finish, the feel — so getting ready is the easiest, best part of your day.
